Search The University of Maryland's Faculty Salary Data

Sort this data by college, faculty position, salary, or search for a specific name.

The cells in the 'Base Annual Salary' column are colored on a gradiant to show how a base annual salary compares across all salaries at this university.

Some faculty members may have more than one search result. This means the faculty member holds multiple positions. To calculate their base annual salary, add up the salaries of each position they have listed.
